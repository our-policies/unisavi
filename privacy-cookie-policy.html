Privacy & Cookie Policy

At UniSavi, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how UniSavi Ltd (UK), together with its subsidiary UniSavi EdTech Ltd (Nigeria), collects, uses, and protects your personal information, as well as how we use cookies to enhance your experience.

1 Introduction

This Privacy & Cookie Policy explains how UniSavi Ltd (UK), together with its subsidiary UniSavi EdTech Ltd (Nigeria), collects, uses, and protects personal information through the UniSavi App.

2 Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:
  • Identity documents: passport, national ID, or other proof of identity
  • Contact details: name, email, phone, address
  • Application data: academic records, certificates, references, supporting documents
  • Technical data: device type, IP address, browser information, app usage statistics
  • Payment records: Transaction details, confirmation of fees processed via third-party providers. We do not store credit card or bank account details

3 How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used to:
  • Process and manage applications to educational institutions.
  • Provide student support and related services.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Improve the functionality of the App.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account and services.

4 Legal Basis for Processing

We process data on the following legal bases:
  • Contractual necessity: To process your applications and deliver services
  • Legal obligation: To comply with immigration, tax, and reporting requirements
  • Consent: For marketing communications where required
  • Legitimate interests: Service improvements, fraud prevention

5 Data Storage, Security & International Transfers

  • Data is primarily stored and processed in Nigeria.
  • Data may be transferred to the UK and other jurisdictions where UniSavi's partners or service providers operate.
  • Where data is transferred internationally, appropriate safeguards (such as contractual clauses) are applied.
  • Payments made through the App are processed via secure third-party providers (e.g., Flutterwave, Flywire). UniSavi does not collect or store card or bank details.
  • Transaction records may include tax information (such as VAT or DST charged) as required for compliance with local tax laws in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, or other applicable jurisdictions. These records are retained only for the period necessary to meet legal and accounting obligations.

6 Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, restricted access, and secure servers.
  • We do not sell personal data.

7 Cookies

The App and its web integrations may use cookies or tracking technologies to improve user experience.
  • Essential cookies enable core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies are used only with consent where required (EU/UK).
